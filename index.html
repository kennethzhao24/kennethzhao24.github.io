Brief Bio

I am a PhD candidate at University of Central Florida, under the advisory of Prof. Jun Wang. My research interests are machine learning systems (MLSys). My goal is to leverage novel algorithm-system co-design methodologies to build efficient, scalabale and susatinable systems for emerging AI applications, such as large language models (LLMs).

I am open to collaborations with both industry and academic researchers (preferrably within U.S.). Email if interested!
